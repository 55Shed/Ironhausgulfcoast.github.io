About The Project

Gulf Coast Housing Built Different

IronHaus Gulf Coast LLC develops high-quality modular housing using repurposed ISO shipping containers — durable, hurricane-resistant, and distinctively coastal.

Shedrick Peters, Managing Member
IronHaus Gulf Coast LLC · Pass Christian, Mississippi

🔩

Cor-Ten Steel Construction

ISO containers naturally resist Gulf Coast salt air, humidity, and hurricane conditions — outlasting wood-frame construction.

🌊

FEMA Elevated Foundation

Reinforced concrete pier foundation on both apartment buildings — elevated above Base Flood Elevation on the Gulf Coast.

🏠

960 SF — Above Market Average

Average Gulf Coast 2-bedroom is 850–950 SF. Our units are larger with 9ft 6in ceilings — commanding premium rents.

Build & Sell Strategy
